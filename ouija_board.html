Rules of the Board

  • Never play alone. The spirits are unpredictable and draw energy from numbers.
  • Never play in an unblessed area, such as a graveyard or site of tragedy.
  • Never ask the board when or how you are going to die. Some questions are not meant to be answered.
  • Always be respectful. Do not mock or provoke anything that comes through to communicate.
  • If the planchette swoops to the four corners of the board, a malevolent spirit has made contact. Close the session immediately.
  • If the planchette begins to count down through the numbers or the alphabet backwards, quickly end the session.
  • Always say "Goodbye" at the end of every session, otherwise the doorway remains perpetually open.

THE SEANCE AWAITS

To establish a connection with the other side, the board must detect the spiritual frequencies in your environment. Please allow access when prompted.
